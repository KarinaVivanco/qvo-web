Supervision of Pucarrajo
Mine Closure Work

📍 Huascarán National Park — Perú · Since July 2024

The general supervision of the mine closure works at the Pucarrajo Mining Unit is currently underway. The mine is located within the Huascarán National Park, an area of high environmental protection in Peru.

This work has been ongoing since July 2024 and includes the supervision of closure for the following main components:

03

05
Tailings Storage Facilities

Concentrator Plant

To carry out this supervision, our General Manager previously worked as General Manager of the former owner company of the Pucarrajo mine, directing the development of the environmental baseline and engineering studies required for the closure designs — including environmental, geotechnical and hydrogeological studies.

This background provided a significant advantage, offering a broad and clear vision of the environmental and long-term stability objectives of the closure, and ensuring the work is carried out within the best engineering practices.
